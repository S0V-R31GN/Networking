NOTE: As of 2026, you almost never implement hubs in real life because they are obsolete and insecure; use a switch instead.

Setup
6 PCs connected via a hub
Assign an IP address to each PC


Connectivity Test (optional)

Click any PC → ping any other PC to confirm they're connected.

Simulation
In Simulation mode:

Give one PC a source packet
Give another PC the destination packet
Start the capture
